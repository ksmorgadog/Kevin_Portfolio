Book Sales - Kaggle

September 23, 2022

The challenge related to this project - competition is the prediction of total sales of four books in six countries. However, the main difficulty is that the years to be predicted include the period of pandemic and quarantine, being necessary to make an adjustment in order to predict the values for the year 2021.

Specifically, the biggest change is in 2020, with a decrease in sales within the quarantine period. Therefore, in order to make the prediction, an XGBoost model and the following time-based variables, given by the Feature Engineering or Feature Creation process, were used:

  • Day of the week
  • Day of the year
  • Month
  • Quarantine period
  • Sine and cosine transformation of the month
  • Important dates such as holidays and other critical events.

With this information I was able to obtain an rsme error of 11 for the training set and 25 for the validation set, divided into a percentage of 90% and 10% with respect to the total available data. Finally, the elements that most influenced the calculation of the value were identified, with the retail store being the most significant according to the XGBoost model report.
